Cuba Libre

Description

A classic Cuba libre recipe made with rum, cola, and a splash of lime.

Does it get any easier than this?

Details

  • Prep time: 5 mins
  • Total time: 5 mins
  • Servings: 1 (one)

Ingredients

  • 4 ice cubes
  • 2 fluid ounces rum
  • 4 fluid ounces cola
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, or to taste

Steps

  1. Fill a 12-ounce glass with ice cubes. Pour rum and cola into the glass. Stir in lime juice and serve.
