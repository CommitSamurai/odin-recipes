Apfelstrudel

Description

This apple strudel recipe is very easy to make with apples, raisins, and frozen puff pastry,

yet it looks impressive and tastes amazing! A truly delicious fall dessert.

Details

Prep time: 20 mins

Cook time: 30 mins

Total time: 50 mins

Servings: 12

Yield: 2 (two) apple strudels
