British Breakfast

Description

The 'common' full English breakfast is a substantial meal consisting of back bacon, eggs, British sausage, baked beans, bubble and squeak, fried tomato, fried mushrooms, black pudding, with fried and toasted bread on the side

Details

  • Prep time: 5 mins
  • Cook time: 20 mins
  • Total time: 25 mins
  • Servings: 1(one)
  • Yield> 1 serving

Ingredients

  • Â¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 link pork sausage
  • 1 frozen hash brown patty
  • 2 thick slices bacon
  • 1 tomato, cut in half
  • 4 mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 slice white bread
  • 1 teaspoon butter, or as needed

Steps

Part 1 (one):

  1. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Add the sausage and hash brown. Fry until browned on one side, about 5 minutes
  3. Turn them over to fry on the other side and add the tomato, bacon and mushrooms
  4. The idea is to start cooking with the things that take the longest.

Part 2

  1. When the tomato, bacon and mushrooms are just about cooked, crack the egg into the center and allow to cook
  2. You might want to add a little more oil just to crisp the edges
  3. Toast the slice of bread while the egg cooks and then spread butter on it
  4. Serve everything on a plate with the toast on the side
